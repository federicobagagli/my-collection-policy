Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2025

This app ("My Collection Manager") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following data:

  • Authentication: We use Firebase Authentication to let you log in via Google or email. We store your user ID but not your password.
  • Database: Your collection data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore, and linked to your account.
  • Ads: We use Google AdMob to show banner and interstitial ads to non-premium users. AdMob may collect device and usage information as per its own privacy policy.
  • Crash Reporting: Firebase Crashlytics is used to monitor crashes and improve app stability. It may collect basic diagnostic data like device model and app version.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for enabling app features, such as saving your collections, restoring backups, and controlling access to premium features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by Firebase and AdMob services.

4. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all related data at any time from within the app's Settings screen.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@example.com
